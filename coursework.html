Diabetic Readmission Prediction

Duration: Mid Mar 2025 – Apr 2025

Designed a pipeline that cleans hospital records, tests hypotheses, engineers risk features, and trains ensemble models to flag patients likely to be readmitted within 30 days. The analysis highlights prior inpatient visits and overall utilization as the strongest signals and discusses fairness trade-offs and threshold choices for clinical use.

Wildlife Strike Analytics

Duration: Mid Mar 2025 – Apr 2025

Built an analysis pipeline that cleans FAA wildlife-strike records, visualizes trends, and tests hypotheses to identify where and when risk concentrates. The study shows highest risk near large coastal airports and migration corridors—especially during approach/landing at ~140–160 kt— and uses a simple trend model (AWP region, 1990–2015) to forecast 2016–2020 while noting limits of linear extrapolation. Findings inform airport-adjacent mitigation and season-aware planning.

FIFA Ticket Market Price Competition

Duration: Nov 2025 – Dec 2025

Modeled price competition between FIFA's official resale platform and the unofficial secondary market using a static Bertrand duopoly framework with a deviation parameter. Estimated a linear demand system from real transaction data, evaluated revenues under four pricing regimes, and visualized comparative statics of equilibrium deviations.

Crime Analysis: Boston & Los Angeles

Duration: Jul 2024 – Sep 2024

Analyzed crime trends in Boston and Los Angeles from 2020 to 2024, focusing on fluctuations during major events such as economic recessions and public health crises.
