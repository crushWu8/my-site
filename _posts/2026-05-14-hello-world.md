Starting a blog feels like buying a new notebook — half the value is the blank page in front of you, and half is the quiet promise that you'll show up to fill it.

I plan to write here about three kinds of things:

- **Math I'm learning** — short notes, worked examples, intuition I wish someone had told me earlier.
- **Data projects** — what I tried, what broke, and the chart that finally made sense.
- **Side observations** — things from soccer, books, faith, anything that quietly shapes how I think.

Posts will be short more often than long. If you want to follow along, there's an [RSS feed]({{ '/feed.xml' | relative_url }}) — or just check back when you're curious.

Thanks for being here.
